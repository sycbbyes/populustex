    CHINA POPULUS TEXTILE LIMITED is the leading company of textile focuses on green, natural, environment friendly and spinning easily raw white materials.

    Beginning from 2000, our team are concentrated on supplying of fashion, soft and comfortable textile materials bamboo fiber, bamboo yarn, bamboo top, viscose fiber, hemp viscose fiber and viscose tow.

    In 2009, we co-worked with our Pakistan partners start to export cotton yarn, acrylic yarn, flax yarn, nylon yarn, organic cotton yarn and viscose yarn to China and other countries.

    In 2012 CHINA POPULUS TEXTILE LIMITED are glad to take a new environmental fiber and yarn into your life Lyocell bamboo fiber and Lyocell bamboo yarn. The special Characteristics of Lyocell bamboo fiber are antibacterial, breathability, suitable to make high-count yarns , good spinnability, excellent dyeing properties and anti-pill. Fabrics made by Lyocell Bamboo are shape-stable and machine-washed. CHINA POPULUS TEXTILE LIMITED as the special supplier of Lyocell bamboo fiber and Lyocell bamboo yarn are proud to make your life more comfortable.
