    B.ACRYLIC YARN

    Acrylic is a synthetic fiber, it is lightweight, soft, and warm, with a wool-like feel. It dyes very well and has excellet colorfastness. Acrylic has recently been used in clothing as a less expensive alternative to cashmere, due to the similar feeling of the materials.

    Applications:
    Apparel: Dresses, infant wear, knitted garments,lingerie, linings, shirts, slacks, sportswear
    Fabrics: Fleece and pile fabrics, face fabrics in bonded fabrics, simulated furs, jerseys.
    Home Furnishings: Blankets, carpets, draperies, upholstery
    Other: industrial and geotextile fabrics

    Characteristics: Soft and warm, Wool-like, Retains shape, Resilient, Quick-drying, Resistant to moths, sunlight, oil and chemicals. Dyes well and has excellent color fastness, Tendency to pill.

    Count range:
    From Ne 30 to Ne 40 in 100% Acrylic
    From Ne 30 to Ne 60 in Acrylic/cotton blends
    From Ne 30 to Ne 60 in Acrylic/viscose blends

    C.FLAX YARN

    Flax fiber is extracted from the bast or skin of the stem of the flax plant. Flax fiber is soft, lustrous and flexible; bundles of fiber have the appearance of blonde hair, hence the description “flaxen”. Flax is grown in many parts of the world., but top quality flax is primarily grown in Ireland, Italy and Belgium.

    Applications:
    Apparel: suits, dresses, skirts, shirts, etc.
    Fabrics: The best grades are used for linen fabrics such as damasks, lace and sheeting
    Home Furnishings: Linen uses range from bed and bath fabrics (tablecloths, dish towels, bed sheets, etc.), home and commercial furnishing items (wallpaper/wall coverings, upholstery, window treatments, etc.),
    Other: industrial products (luggage, canvases, sewing thread, etc

    Cotton/flax blend crate yarns which will improve the feel of denim during hot and humid weather. Characteristics: Highly absorbent and a good conductor of heat. Linen fabric feel cool to the touch. Linen fabrics have a high natural luster; their natural color ranges between shades of stiff fabric and is less likely to cling to the skin; I t is a very durable, strong fabric, and one of the few that are stronger wet than dry. It is resistant to moths and carpet beetles. It resists dirt and stains. Has no lint or pilling tendency. A characteristic often associated with contemporary linen yarn is the presence of “slubs”, or small knots which occur randomly along its length.

    Count rang:

    From Ne 10 to Ne 30 in Flax/Cotton.
    Flax/Viscose or Flax/Polyester blend

    Blending rati of Flax varies from 5% to 55%.

    D. NYLON YARN

    Nylon is a strong, light-synthetic fiber. Nylon thread is made from polymerization of an amine and an acid chloride. Nylon is popular for hosiery and is used to make track pants, swimwear, wind breakers, bed spreads and draperies.

    Applications:
    Carpet fiber, Apparel, Bed linen, Airbags, Tires, Ropes

    Characteristics:
    Nylon had the ability to be very lustrous semi-lustrous or dull.
    Durability: its high tenacity fibers are sued for seatbelts, tire cords, ballistic cloth and other uses.
    High elongation
    Excellent abrasion resistance
    Highly resilient (nylon fabrics are heat-set)
    Paved the way for easy-care garments
    High resistance to:
    Insects, fungi and animals
    Molds, mildew, rot
    Many chemicals
    Melts instead of burning
    Used in carpets and nylon stockings
    Used in many military applications

    Count rang:
    From Ne 10 to 20 in following blends:
    100% Nylon, Cotton/Nylon, Pllyester/Nylon.

    E.ORGANIC COTTON YARN

    Organic cotton yarns are produced with a fiber naturally sown and
    grown in fields that haven’t been chemically treated for almost
    three years. Organic yarns allow producing manufactured goods
    meeting the request of ecologically involved consumer. The production
    is scrutinized from the cotton field to the spinning mill and finally
    certified by Control Union. We are certified with Control
    Union bearing certification# CU 807868.

    Applications:
    Bed lined, Intimate Apparel, Baby wear, High grade woven
    fabrics for shirts & blouses.

    Characteristics:
    Comfort, performance and naturalness. Extra resistance to rubbing
    and pilling. Better definition and brightness of colors. Soft, silky touch. Longer lasting and pleasant to wear. Ideal for “non-iron”fabrics.

    Count range: From Ne 10 to Ne 100 both knitting & weaving
    Available also in S and Z twist.
