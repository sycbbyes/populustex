    • 2/16/2012                             Pakistani Textile Market Report                                               
    巴基斯坦纺织市场报告
    Cotton Prices slowly decreaces in last week, not only in Pakistan but internationally trend observed.
    上周巴基斯坦棉花的价格稍有降幅，整个棉花国际市场也是同样的趋势。
    From last 2 months in Pakistan Energy crisis keep hitting the Textile sector in shape of insufficient  production which merely meet the local & international yarn demand.
    前两个月，巴基斯坦的能源危机冲击了纺织行业导致生产不足，仅够维持本地市场和国际市场的需求。
    But now Situation much better & Energy restore & Units are running in full capacity result in full production. So Yarn Prices becoming soft & business activities start.
    但是现在这种情况有所好转，能源供应恢复了，各厂生产线全部开工，所以纱线价格维稳，市场开始活跃。
    Market Expert says that due slow economic growth in united states & Europe, less Export of Textiles & Apparel could hit cotton demand more in coming days. Prices expected more ease.
    市场专家预测近期由于美国和欧洲的经济增速放缓，纺织出口量的减少，成衣市场对棉花需求的刺激减少，巴基斯坦纱线价格将呈下降趋势。
